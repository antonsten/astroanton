<p>Even most humans have trouble understanding and adapting to local culture of driving. Driving in Sweden is very different from driving in just Italy or France. The Middle East or even the US is something entirely different too. Thinking that driving patterns for a self-driving car could be linear is as naive as thinking that every user of your product thinks and acts in the same way.</p>
