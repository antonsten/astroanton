<a href="https://twitter.com/lisalindstrm">Lisa</a> is a powerhouse. It's clear how the practises the founders of Doberman were taught at <a href="/no-classes">Hyper Island</a> are reflected in how they choose to run Doberman. Transparency, honesty and trust.
