>But when you go out to dinner with a group of friends and the subject of the internet comes up, the focus isn’t on social media’s invasion of privacy. No one is getting a little buzzed and saying, _“My profile is being unethically shared to target me with ads that purport to know what I want. Now give me another drink.”_**<a href="https://medium.com/@davepell/the-real-technology-problem-bc6aa27e526b">The Real Technology Problem</a>**

This post hits home with me on so many levels. The problem isn’t Facebook—it’s us. We’re so addicted to technology that privacy has become an afterthought.
